Using the Board Editor

This website mimics a physical chess board. As such, it is simple and intuitive to use. There are two parts: the board itself, and the replacement table.

Chessboard

The chessboard is the grid of 64 red and white squares with pieces in the middle of the screen. To select a piece, click on its square. To move it, simply click on another square on the board. The piece will be moved to that square. If there is another piece on the square, that piece will be replaced.

Replacement Table

The replacement table is located beneath the chess board. Like on the board, click on a piece to select it. When you click on a square on the board, the piece will be replicated there (unlike on the chessboard, the original piece on the replacement table is not removed. To remove pieces from the chessboard, first click on the red X on the replacement table, then click on the piece.
